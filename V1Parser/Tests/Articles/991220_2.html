Top 10 2.5G and 3G Subscriber Markets, 2006

Broader-band wireless access, often called third generation (3G) and 2.5G service, will lead the way to wider use of smart phones and other wireless Net devices.  The Strategis Group says that countries in Asia and Europe will lead the way to 2.5G and 3G adoption.

(Millions of subscribers)

Country 2.5G Subscribers in 2006 (millions) 3G Subscribers in 2006 (millions)
China 48.9 26.3
Japan 14.4 21.5
Germany 16 8.6
U.K. 14.7 7.9
France 12.3 6.6
Italy 10.5 5.7
United States 11.1 2.8
Spain 7.6 4.1
Korea 5.3 5.3
Australia 3.9 2.1

Source: The Strategis Group, Inc.
