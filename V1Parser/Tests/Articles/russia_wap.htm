WAP Trials Start In Russia

December 17, 1999 -- Motorola and Mobile TeleSystems CJSC (MTS) have started trials on the first Wireless Application Protocol (WAP)-enabled wireless system in Russia.

MTS is Russia's largest GSM wireless phone service operator, with a subscriber base of about 340,000.  In the trials, WAP-enabled phones are accessing Internet portals such as Yahoo!.   

The company says the service will be available commercially in early 2000.
